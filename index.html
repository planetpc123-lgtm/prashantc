Late Raobahadur Damodar Neelkantha Alias Annasaheb Khare  and his six  colleagues founded Wardha Education Society and started New English High School with 14 students in 1914. 

Open air theatre was created through the continuous efforts of our then teacher and the famous dramatist Late Shri. M.D. Deshmukh and some staff members of the school which is the centre of cultural programmes in Wardha.The Golden jubilee of the institution was enthusiastically celebrated in 1968. Wardha Education Society Wardha started junior College in 1975. It has just completed its centenary year in 2014 under the auspicious presence of Late honourable Dr. APJ kalam. As it is rightly said "Old is Gold" so is the Wardha Education Society is still shinning.
